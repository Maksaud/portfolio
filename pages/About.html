About Me

My Story

I'm Maksaud Ahmed, but you can call me Maksaud

Grew up in London where, at a very early age of 7, I was interested in tech the day my dad bought me a computer. Instead of using it to play flash games or do "homework", I damaged it with curiosity. I wanted to know what makes a computer work.

After I built my first PC at the age of 15, I got more curious and wanted to understand what all these computer parts do. After reading a book called "But How Do It Know?" by J.Clark Scott, I found that bits are being passed around as of instructions and thought of what could these lines of code be, what can be done with them.

I have since been honing my skills and getting better over the years using my natural problem-solving skills. Now I want to use these skills to create elegant solutions and delight users.

I have worked with scrum teams and agile teams in the past which allowed me to meet new people, work with them, trying to learn from them and I would like to continue to do so.
